The Innovation And Entrepreneurship
Development Centres(IEDC)

Vision

The main objective of NSS programme is to prepare the NSS volunteers for the democratic, self-disciplined and self-reliant way of life



Mission

  • IEDC mainly focused on fostering of innovative ideas of students and also provide both the technical and financial support to implement them.
  • IEDC is providing an Entrepreneurial eco system in the campus.
  • IEDC is also providing additional technical skill training to its members.



Introduction

Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) is an agency under the Kerala government working to develop an entrepreneurship culture by promoting Startups and incubating activities. Innovation And Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) are Companies which works as facilitators to achieve the vision of KSUM.



IEDC Organization


IEDC mainly focused on fostering of innovative ideas of students and also provide both the technical and financial support to implement them. IEDC is providing an Entrepreneurial eco system in the campus. IEDC is also providing additional technical skill training its members.



Why IEDC

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centres (IEDC) are platforms set up in Engineering, Management, Arts & Science Colleges and Polytechnics with an aim to provide students an opportunity to experiment and innovate. Kerala Startup Mission has set up IEDCs in 226 institutions across the State which provide avenues for creative students to learn, collaborate and transform their innovative ideas into prototypes of viable products and services. IEDCs works as the first launch pad for a student’s entrepreneurial journey and provide them with access to cutting edge technology, world class infrastructure, high quality mentorship, early risk capital and global exposure.
